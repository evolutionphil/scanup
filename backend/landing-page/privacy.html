Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

ScanUp ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Key Point: Your documents are stored locally on your device by default. We only access your data when you explicitly use cloud features.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and name (if you create an account)
  • Documents: Scanned documents (stored locally on your device)
  • Signatures: Digital signatures you create
  • Support Requests: Information you provide when contacting us

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: App features used, interaction patterns
  • Crash Reports: Technical data to help us fix bugs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Sync your documents across devices (if you enable cloud sync)
  • Send important updates about the app
  • Improve our app and user experience
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: By default, all documents are stored only on your device
  • Cloud Sync: If enabled, documents are encrypted before upload
  • Encryption: We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption
  • Access Control: Only you can access your documents
Security Note: We never sell your personal data or documents to third parties.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: Export all your documents
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Correct: Update or correct your personal information

6. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Analytics: To understand app usage (anonymized data)
  • Cloud Storage: For optional backup features (AWS/Google Cloud)
  • Payment Processing: Handled by app stores (Apple/Google)

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. International Data Transfers

If you use our cloud features, your data may be processed in data centers located in the United States or European Union, with appropriate safeguards in place.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
