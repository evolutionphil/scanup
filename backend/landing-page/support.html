Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a document?
  1. Open ScanUp and tap the blue scan button at the bottom
  2. Point your camera at the document you want to scan
  3. The app will automatically detect the edges and capture
  4. Adjust the crop if needed, then apply filters
  5. Tap "Save" to save your document

Pro tip: Use good lighting and hold your phone steady for best results!

How do I add multiple pages to a document?
  1. Scan your first page as normal
  2. Instead of saving, tap "Add Page" button
  3. Scan additional pages
  4. Arrange pages by dragging them into order
  5. Tap "Save" when you've added all pages
How do I sign a document?
  1. Open the document you want to sign
  2. Tap the "Sign" button in the toolbar
  3. Select an existing signature or create a new one
  4. Position and resize your signature
  5. Tap "Apply" to add the signature

You can create and save multiple signatures for different purposes.

How do I share a document?
  1. Open the document you want to share
  2. Tap the share button
  3. Choose your format (PDF or Image)
  4. Select your sharing method (Email, Messages, AirDrop, etc.)
How do I backup my documents?

Your documents are automatically saved on your device. For cloud backup:

  1. Create an account or sign in
  2. Go to Settings > Backup
  3. Enable "Auto Backup"
  4. Your documents will sync automatically

Premium users get unlimited cloud storage.

How do I upgrade to Premium?
  1. Go to Settings > Premium
  2. Choose your plan (Monthly, Yearly, or Lifetime)
  3. Complete the purchase through the App Store or Google Play

Premium features include unlimited scans, no watermark, cloud backup, and digital signatures.

How do I restore my purchases?
  1. Go to Settings > Premium
  2. Tap "Restore Purchases"
  3. Sign in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase

Your purchases are linked to your app store account, not your ScanUp account.
