Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ScanUp. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

Getting Started

How do I scan my first document?

Open the ScanUp app and tap the camera button at the bottom. Point your camera at the document - our AI will automatically detect the edges. Once captured, you can adjust the crop, apply filters, and save as PDF.

Is ScanUp available on both iOS and Android?

Yes! ScanUp is available on both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. The app works identically on both platforms, and your documents sync across all your devices.

Do I need an account to use ScanUp?

You can use basic scanning features without an account. However, creating a free account enables cloud backup, cross-device sync, and access to the web dashboard.

Features

How does auto edge detection work?

Our AI-powered edge detection uses computer vision to identify document boundaries automatically. It works on any background and can detect documents at various angles.

Can I add digital signatures to documents?

Yes! Premium users can create and save multiple signatures. Draw your signature once, then apply it to any document with a single tap. Signatures are stored securely on your device.

What export formats are supported?

You can export documents as PDF (recommended for sharing), JPEG, or PNG. Multi-page documents are automatically combined into a single PDF file.

Web Dashboard

What is the Web Dashboard?

The Web Dashboard lets you access and manage your scanned documents from any web browser. You can view, organize into folders, rename, download as PDF, and delete documents - all from your computer.

How do I access the Web Dashboard?

Visit scanup.app/dashboard and sign in with your account. You can use email/password or Google Sign-In. Your documents will sync automatically.

Can I create folders on the web?

Yes! You can create custom folders with different colors to organize your documents. Simply drag and drop documents into folders, or use the "Move to Folder" button.

Do changes on web sync to my phone?

Absolutely! All changes sync in real-time. Rename a document on the web, and it updates on your phone. Create a folder on your phone, and it appears on the web dashboard.

Subscription & Pricing

What's included in the free version?

Free users get 10 scans per month, basic filters, PDF export (with watermark), and local storage. It's perfect for occasional scanning needs.

What does Pro subscription include?

Pro ($4.99/month) includes unlimited scans, all premium filters, digital signatures, cloud backup, no watermarks, OCR text recognition, and priority support.

Is there a lifetime purchase option?

Yes! For $49.99 one-time, you get all Pro features forever with lifetime updates. It's the best value if you plan to use ScanUp long-term.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). Go to your device's subscription settings to cancel. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Privacy & Security

Are my documents stored securely?

Yes! All data is encrypted using AES-256 at rest and TLS/SSL in transit. We follow industry best practices and use SOC 2 compliant cloud infrastructure.

Can I password-protect sensitive documents?

Premium users can add password protection to individual documents or folders. The password is required to view or export the document.

Does ScanUp sell my data?

Never. We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal data or documents. Your documents are yours alone. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
