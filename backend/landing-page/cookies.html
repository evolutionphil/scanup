Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our services.

Types of Cookies We Use

TypePurposeDuration
EssentialRequired for basic website functionality, login sessionsSession
AuthenticationKeep you logged in securely7 days
PreferencesRemember your settings (theme, language)1 year
AnalyticsUnderstand how visitors use our website2 years

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function. They include:

  • scanup_token: Authentication token for logged-in users
  • session_id: Maintains your session state

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics to improve our services. These cookies help us understand:

  • Which pages are most popular
  • How users navigate through our site
  • Technical issues users may encounter

We do NOT use analytics to track individual users or collect personal information.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication
  • Stripe: For payment processing (if applicable)

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
