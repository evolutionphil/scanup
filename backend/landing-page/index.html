FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ScanUp

Is ScanUp free to use?

Yes! ScanUp offers a free version with unlimited scans and no watermarks. Premium features like advanced OCR, cloud sync, and batch processing are available with a subscription starting at just 4.99/month.

What file formats does ScanUp support?

ScanUp supports PDF and JPEG formats for export. You can scan documents and export them as multi-page PDFs or individual high-quality JPEG images. All exports maintain the original quality of your scans.

Can I scan multiple pages into one document?

Absolutely! ScanUp allows you to scan multiple pages and combine them into a single PDF document. You can easily reorder, delete, rotate, or add new pages at any time before exporting.

Does ScanUp work offline?

Yes, ScanUp works completely offline! You can scan, edit, organize, and export documents without an internet connection. Cloud sync is available when you're online to backup and access your documents across devices.

Is my data secure with ScanUp?

Your privacy is our top priority. ScanUp uses industry-standard encryption to protect your documents. We never access, share, or sell your personal documents. All data is stored securely and you have full control over your files.

Can I add digital signatures to documents?

Yes! ScanUp includes a powerful digital signature feature. You can draw your signature with your finger, save multiple signatures, and add them to any document with a simple tap. Perfect for contracts, forms, and official documents.

Does ScanUp have OCR (text recognition)?

Yes, ScanUp includes advanced OCR technology powered by AI. It can recognize and extract text from scanned documents in over 50 languages. You can copy, search, and edit the extracted text easily.

What devices is ScanUp available on?

ScanUp is available on both iOS (iPhone and iPad) and Android devices. Download it free from the App Store or Google Play Store. Your documents sync seamlessly across all your devices with cloud backup.
